Introduction

Thank you for using PixelPick. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

PixelPick does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Our application operates entirely on your device, and we do not:

  • Collect any personally identifiable information
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Store any data on our servers
  • Access your contacts, location, or other personal information
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Share any information with third parties

How the App Works

PixelPick processes all information locally on your device. Any images or data you work with remain on your device and are not transmitted to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

PixelPick does not integrate with or use any third-party services that would collect your data.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no data for us to protect on our end. All processing happens locally on your device, and the security of that data depends on your device's security measures.

Children's Privacy

Our application does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact information provided on our main website.

Summary: PixelPick respects your privacy by not collecting any personal data whatsoever. Everything happens locally on your device.
